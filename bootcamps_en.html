Alternative Learning

EGADE Bootcamps

EGADE Bootcamps offers intensive and practical training programs centered around cutting-edge areas, which are delivered either on-site or on a virtual reality platform.

Follow a competency-based learning model and learn from mentors who are global experts in their respective fields. These experts will walk you through the development of a project and/or prototype that will later be reviewed by a panel of judges.

Entrepreneurship Bootcamp

Experience an intensive program and learn the best strategies to build your vision from the ground up. This Bootcamp will teach you the right methodology to launch your business, as well as the basics and everything you need to know to get started on the right track.

This program is for people who want to optimize their time and work in high-intensity teams while receiving support and feedback from global experts. At the end of this Bootcamp, you will be able to have a 360° view of your project.

20 hours
Info
Future Thinking Bootcamp

Learn about future thinking methodologies, case studies, and innovative strategies that have led large organizations and trailblazers to disruptive innovation.

At the end of this Bootcamp, you will create a prototype based on what you have learned. This prototype will be a proposal on how to handle a scenario under uncertain circumstances. By designing this proposal you will have a better understanding of how to react and prepare for different scenarios that will lead to many business opportunities.
